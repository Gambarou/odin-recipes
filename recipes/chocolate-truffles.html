Chocolate Truffles Recipe

Chocolate Truffles

Makes 12 truffles. 45 minutes prep time. For a decadent chocolate treat, try this luxurious recipe for Chocolate Truffles. The perfect gift for family and friends!

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g double cream
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 250g 66% Valrhona chocolate
  • Cocoa powder

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Break up the chocolate into a bowl.
  2. Add the cream and the vanilla pod to a saucepan and bring to the boil.
  3. When the mixture is boiling, pass the cream through a fine sieve onto the chocolate.
  4. Whisk the mixture until the chocolate has melted and is shiny.
  5. Chill the mixture in the fridge.
  6. When cold, roll it into small, truffle sized, balls.
  7. Sppon out some cocoa powder onto a plate and roll the balls in the cocoa powder, completely covering them.
  8. Alternatively, roll the truffles in chopped nuts, crushed cocoa nibs, icing sugar, or freeze them and dip into melted white chocolate.
  9. Eat immediately or store in the fridge, these make a great edible gift, too.
